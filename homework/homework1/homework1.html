Brownie recipe page

This is my recipe for making delicious brownies

Steps required below

    The Secret Recipe

  1. Grease & line your baking tin with baking paper then preheat your oven to 180°C/Fan 170°C.

  2. Add the butter into a large bowl and break in the chocolate.
    Melt in the microwave in 20 second intervals, stirring well between each time. Try not to overheat.
    Leave to one side for a few minutes to cool to room temperature.

  3. One at a time, beat in the eggs. Mix very well.

  4. Add in the sugar and give it a really good beating with a spatula or whisk. It should stop being really grainy and become quite smooth.

  5. Stir through the vanilla & salt then sift in the flour & cocoa powder.
    Fold it all together with a spatula but don’t overdo it. Fold just until you can’t see anymore flour left in the batter.

  6. Pour the lot into your prepared tin and smooth out evenly. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes.
    It will likely be a little crispy on top but fear not, there is lots of gorgeous chocolatey goo underneath!

  7. The pegs? Yes, that’s what happens when you’re lazy and only line the base & 2 sides of your tin. The paper falls onto the brownie & creates unsightly lines on the top until you notice and peg it back up. Oops.

    Anyway! Allow to cool completely before removing from the tin and slicing into 9 or 16 bars.
